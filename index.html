Understanding GST (Goods and Services Tax)

GST is an indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services. It is charged at every stage, from the manufacturer to the consumer.

Manufacturer
Wholesaler
Retailer
Consumer

Manufacturer: The manufacturer produces goods and pays GST on the raw materials. When they sell the goods to the wholesaler, they charge GST on the final product.

Wholesaler: The wholesaler buys from the manufacturer and adds GST when selling to the retailer.

Retailer: The retailer purchases goods from the wholesaler and charges GST when selling to the final consumer.

Consumer: The consumer pays the final GST, which includes the tax charged at each stage.
